Frequently Asked Questions

How can I check my warranty status?

You can check your warranty status by visiting our support page and entering your device's serial number. The serial number can be found in Settings > General > About on your device.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, Apple Pay, PayPal, and financing options through Apple Card Monthly Installments.

How do I schedule a Genius Bar appointment?

You can schedule an appointment through our website or the Apple Support app. Select your preferred store location and available time slot.
